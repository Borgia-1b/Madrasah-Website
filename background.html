Background and Significance of our project proposal


Homepage ⎮ SIP ⎮ Background ⎮ MAPEH



The project addresses the essential needs of our partnered institution by making baked goods to sell so that we can use the funds to donate it to them. This project empowers them because this can help them in finishing their school and have funds for the materials that they need for the students to use in their academics. This will contribute ultimately to forming a community of productive citizens of the country by being more efficient in terms of business making and how it can benefit the partnered institutions current problems and how it can also benefit the students that go to madrasah to learn. The significance of our project proposal is the enrichment of the problems that our partnered institution is currently facing and how it can be solved.
